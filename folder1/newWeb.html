NiTi TPMS · LPBF
Imperfection formation in thin NiTi TPMS layers

Investigated balling, cracking, and spatter in NiTi Schwarz TPMS layers fabricated via LPBF at varying relative densities and scan strategies, linking melt pool behavior to surface quality and microstructure.

NiTi · LPBF Process–defect mapping Thin-wall lattices
Outcome: Guidelines to reduce balling & improve lattice integrity.
Architected materials
Microstructure in NiTi TPMS lattices

Fabricated Primitive and Gyroid NiTi TPMS lattices via LPBF and analyzed Ni evaporation, oxide formation, and phase distribution as a function of geometry and process parameters.

TPMS design Microstructure control Functional applications
Outcome: Design rules for robust NiTi lattice architectures.
Conference · ASME IMECE
Inhomogeneous microstructure in NiTi TPMS

Presented work on non-uniform solidification in NiTi TPMS structures, highlighting how complex geometries influence melt pool dynamics and microstructural gradients.

ASME IMECE 2022 Process–geometry interaction Microstructure gradients
Outcome: Insight into geometry-driven microstructure variation.
