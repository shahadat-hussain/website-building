About My Work

Based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, I bridge the gap between computational models and physical reality. My core research investigates the complex behaviors of Triply Periodic Minimal Surface (TPMS) structures, focusing on the microstructural analysis of NiTi lattices fabricated via Laser Powder Bed Fusion (LPBF). By leveraging Data Science and AI alongside advanced manufacturing, I solve complex engineering challenges for researchers and industry partners alike.

Research Focus

Metamaterials & TPMS Lattices

Designing sub-wavelength architectures, specifically Primitive and Gyroid topologies, to create structures that are incredibly light yet exhibit ultra-stiffness and unique mechanical behaviors.

Shape Memory Alloys (SMAs)

Exploring the temperature-dependent phase transformations of Nickel-Titanium (Nitinol) alloys. Focusing on shape recovery, superelasticity, and solid-state actuation for functional engineering applications.

Microstructural Analysis

Investigating the influence of laser process parameters and lattice geometries on the melt pool dynamics, solidification rates, and inhomogeneous microstructures in 3D-printed metal parts.
